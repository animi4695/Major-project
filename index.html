About

Feature specific product recommendations by analysing customer review

The development and wide adoption of internet have transformed the mode of operation of many businesses, one of which is E-Commerce. E-Commerce has facilitated consumers to shop wide range of products which are available at different locations on a single platform. Due to wide range of products , the quality of the products cannot be guaranteed sometimes. To offer a better service, E-commerce companies facilitate the consumers to post reviews on products. However the number of reviews are more and are also based on complete product which is difficult for a consumer to go through all of them while his interest lies on a particular feature of that product.
It becomes challenging to determine the advantages and disadvantages to different parts/features of a product based on individual reviews. It is critical to identify the sentiment of a particular product based on consumer reviews and categorize opinion/sentiments about different parts/features of a product , since there might be consumers who prefer purchasing product for a particular feature.
Sentiment Analysis or Opinion mining refers to the use of Natural Language Processing, text analysis to identify and extract subjective information from source material(large datasets). It is the computational study of people’s emotions, opinions, attitude towards a particular entity. For example, Consider a review “I like the Samsung j7 modelqHDdisplay but the battery life is worst.”. The above sentence has mixed emotions. The emotion regarding battery life is ‘negative’. Hence it is of utmost importance to extract only those opinions which are relevant. Therefore this project aims to analyse and use Natural Language Processing techniques to retrieve various opinions from an E-commerce dataset(Amazon). Upon retrieval reviews are classified using statistical analysis based on the features of the product and provide a better feedback.
